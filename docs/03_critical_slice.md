# Critical Slice

The quaternionic critical slice is

```text
Re(q)=1/2.
```

It is the proposed equilibrium slice behind the complex critical line
`Re(s)=1/2`.

## Projection To The Complex Critical Line

Choose a unit imaginary quaternion `u`. The slice

```text
q = a + t u
```

is identified with the complex variable

```text
s = a + it.
```

Under this identification, the quaternionic slice condition `Re(q)=1/2`
projects to

```text
Re(s)=1/2.
```

## Equilibrium Interpretation

The conjecture interprets `Re(q)=1/2` as the place where the lifted symmetry is
balanced. The real coordinate is fixed at the center of the `s <-> 1-s`
reflection, while the imaginary orientation records spectral motion.

## Required Structure

- which conjugations preserve the slice,
